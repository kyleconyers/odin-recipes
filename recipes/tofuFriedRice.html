tofu fried rice

ingredients

  • rice
  • vegetable oil
  • tofu
  • broccli
  • salt

instructions

  1. boil rice in water until cooked
  2. spread butter on skillet
  3. chop broccli and tofu
  4. cook broccli and tofu in skillet
  5. once cooked add cooded rice and stir
  6. move to bowl and add salt as desired
